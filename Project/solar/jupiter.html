Jupiter, the fifth planet from the Sun, is the largest planet in our solar system. Its atmosphere is composed mostly of hydrogen and helium, and it has a giant storm, the Great Red Spot, that has raged for centuries. Jupiter is surrounded by dozens of moons, including the four largest—Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto—discovered by Galileo Galilei. Its powerful magnetic field and extensive system of rings add to its majestic presence.
