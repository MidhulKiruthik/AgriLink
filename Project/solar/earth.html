Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only planet known to support life. Its atmosphere, rich in oxygen and nitrogen, provides the perfect environment for life to thrive. Approximately 71% of Earth’s surface is covered with water, making it unique among the solar system’s planets. The planet rotates on its axis every 24 hours, giving rise to day and night, and orbits the Sun in 365.25 days. Earth’s magnetic field and atmosphere protect it from harmful solar radiation, allowing it to sustain a diverse range of ecosystems and species.
