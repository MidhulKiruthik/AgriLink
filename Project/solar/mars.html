Mars, the fourth planet from the Sun, is known as the "Red Planet" due to its iron oxide-rich soil. It is a cold, desert-like world with a thin atmosphere mostly made up of carbon dioxide. Mars has the largest volcano in the solar system, Olympus Mons, and a deep canyon system, Valles Marineris. Several missions have studied Mars, searching for signs of water and life. ISRO’s Mars Orbiter Mission (MOM), or Mangalyaan, successfully orbited Mars in 2014, marking a significant achievement in India’s space exploration history.
